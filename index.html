make a
paper crane

step one.

fold square paper in half diagonally.

step two.

fold in half again from right to left.

step three.

spread the pocket out from the inside and press to make a small square.

step four.

your paper should now look like this. flip over to start next step.

step five.

repeat fold on this side.

step six.

fold sides of top layer to middle and unfold. lift top layer upwards.

step seven.

pinch sides in to create a boat-like shape and flatten. flip over to start next step.

step eight.

repeat fold on this side.

step nine.

fold sides of top layer to middle. flip over and repeat.

step ten.

reverse fold the bottom points.

step eleven.

reverse fold the top of one point.

step twelve.

crease the wings.
